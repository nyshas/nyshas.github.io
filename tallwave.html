Tallwave Website Redesign

Worked On Wireframes, High Fidelity Mockups, Mobile Design
Company Tallwave

During my internship with digital marketing agency Tallwave, I transformed the design of their existing site. I redesigned the Careers, About, Press, and Insights pages and helped construct a new brand feel.

First, I worked in Sketch to construct high fidelity design comps which were reviewed through Invision by other design team members and Tallwave management.

Afterwards, I continued to improved to edit and revise these designed based off content and design feedback from the Invision review stage.

Currently, my design work can be seen on Tallwave's live site.
