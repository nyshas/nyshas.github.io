Mattress Inquirer Rebrand and Redesign

Worked On Design and Branding
Company Mattress Inquirer

Mattress Inquirer began as a website with a standard WordPress layout that quickly became outdated. Seeking to bring the site back to life, I led the full rebrand and redesign from ideation to wireframing to building high fidelity mockups. Stage one of the rebrand involved sketching out logo ideas and revising based on the team's feedback.

After the logo creation and constructing a style guide, I used Sketch and Invision to build out mockups and prototypes that were intensively reviewed by the team in an iterative process. After the design's approval, I worked with the development team to transition the project.
